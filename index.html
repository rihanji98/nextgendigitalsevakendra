AKSAR POOCHE JAANE WALE SAWAAL

Aapke Sawaal, Humaare Jawab

Koi bhi confusion yahan door karein

PAN card kitne din mein banta hai?+
Aam taur par PAN card apply karne ke 7–15 working days mein mil jaata hai. Hum aapko tracking number denge jisse aap khud bhi track kar sakte hain.
Kya ghar baithe apply kar sakte hain?+
Haan! Aap WhatsApp par documents bhej sakte hain — 9149383541. Hum aapko bata denge kya kya chahiye aur payment online ho sakta hai.
Payment kaise karein — UPI ya Cash?+
Aap cash, UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), ya bank transfer — kisi bhi tarike se payment kar sakte hain. Har payment par pakka receipt milega.
Aadhaar update mein kitna samay lagta hai?+
Aadhaar update same day process hota hai hamare kendra par. UIDAI approval mein 2–3 din lag sakte hain lekin hamara kaam usi din hoga.
AEPS kya hai aur kaise kaam karta hai?+
AEPS (Aadhaar Enabled Payment System) se aap apne bank account se Aadhaar fingerprint ke zariye paise deposit ya withdraw kar sakte hain. Koi ATM card ki zaroorat nahi.
PM Awas Yojana ke liye kaun apply kar sakta hai?+
Jo parivar BPL (gareeb rekha se neeche) hain aur jinke paas pakka ghar nahi hai woh apply kar sakte hain. Aadhaar card aur ration card zaroori hai.
